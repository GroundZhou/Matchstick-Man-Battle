# Matchstick Man Battle


A simple pygame.

### Operation

##### Player 1
|key    |action   |
|:-----:|:-------------|
|w      |Up       |
|a      |Left     |
|s      |Down     |
|d      |Right    |
|f      |Attack 1 |
|g      |Attack 2 |


##### Player 2
|key    |action   |
|:-----:|:-------------|
|↑      |Up       |
|←      |Left     |
|↓      |Down     |
|→      |Right    |
|j      |Attack 1 |
|k      |Attack 2 |
